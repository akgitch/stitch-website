Frequently Asked Questions

How do I renew my broadband plan?

You can renew your plan by logging into the ACN Portal or using our mobile app. Select 'Renew Now', choose your preferred plan, and complete the UPI or card payment. Renewal is instant once payment is confirmed.

Where can I check my plan expiry date?

Your plan details, including data usage and expiry date, are visible on the 'Home' dashboard of your account. We also send SMS reminders 3 days and 1 day before your plan expires.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major payment methods including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and other UPI apps. You can also pay using Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay) and Net Banking for all major Indian banks.

How do I report a network outage?

First, check the 'Network Status' tab on our website. If there's no known outage in your area, please restart your router. If the issue persists, send 'OUTAGE' to our WhatsApp support number or call our toll-free line.
